Hi, I'm Mon!

Welcome to Made by Mon Co - a little stationery shop filled with things that make everyday life a bit more cute and colorful.

Our story

Made by Mon Co started as a passion project born from a love of all things cute, kawaii, and crafty. What began as a small Etsy shop has grown into a full stationery brand loved by journalers, scrapbookers, and sticker collectors everywhere.

What we sell

From adorable bow and button sticker sheets to cottagecore junk journal kits, gingham easter stickers to Y2K retro decals - there is something here for every mood, season, and aesthetic.

Made with love

Every order is hand-packed by me, Mon. I care deeply about making sure your goodies arrive safely, beautifully wrapped, and ready to brighten your day. Thank you so much for supporting a small business.

Mental health matters

Made by Mon Co is also a space for self love and healing. Our affirmation stickers are designed with eating disorder recovery and mental health in mind.

FAQs

Everything you need to know before you shop

How long does shipping take? ▾
All orders are processed within 1-3 business days. Once shipped, domestic orders typically arrive within 3-7 business days.
Do you ship internationally? ▾
Yes! We ship worldwide. International shipping times vary, typically 7-21 business days. Customs fees may apply.
What is your return policy? ▾
We do not accept returns unless an item arrives damaged or incorrect. Please reach out within 7 days of delivery and we will make it right!
Can I cancel or change my order? ▾
Orders can be cancelled or changed within 24 hours of purchase. Reach out as soon as possible and we will do our best!
How are payments processed? ▾
All payments are securely processed through Stripe. We accept all major credit and debit cards.
Can I find you on Etsy or TikTok? ▾
Yes! Shop us at MadebymonCo on Etsy and follow @made_by_mon on TikTok for new drops and behind-the-scenes content!
